Account & Data Deletion

If you would like to permanently delete your Progress Pals account and all associated data (including your habits and friend lists), please send an email to support@lesetja.dev with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please ensure you send this from the email address associated with your account so we can verify your identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a friend?
Navigate to the Friends tab, tap the "+" icon, and enter your friend's exact account email address.

Why can't I see my friend's habits?
Ensure your friend has explicitly shared that specific habit with your email address in their habit settings.
