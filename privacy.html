Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Progress Pals ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address and name via Google Firebase Authentication to secure your account. We also store habit data and progress logs in Google Firestore to provide our core tracking features.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide habit tracking and social accountability features. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We utilize industry-standard encryption (AES-256 and TLS) provided by Google Firebase to ensure your data is protected during transmission and at rest.

4. Contact Us

For support or data deletion requests, contact us at: support@lesetja.dev
